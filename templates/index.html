Quast

A quality assesment tool


Quast evaluates genome assemblies by computing various metrics:

  • N50, length of the shortest contig from all the longer contigs that cover 50% of all assembly
  • NG50, where the reference genome is being covered
  • NA50 and NGA50, where aligned blocks instead of contigs are taken
  • Number of open reading frames
  • Genes and operons covered
  • Misassemblies, misassembled and unaligned contigs or contigs bases

Builds convenient plots:

  • for cumulative contigs length
  • all kinds of N-metrics
  • genes and operons fully covered
  • GC content
